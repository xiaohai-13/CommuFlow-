# Internal Communication FAQ

## Q: How to report cross-department issues?
Use Feishu group with all relevant department heads. Escalate to director level if unresolved in 24 hours.

## Q: What is the standard procurement lead time?
Standard: 3 working days. Emergency: green channel via procurement director, same-day processing.

## Q: Quality inspection standard
AQL 1.0 sampling. Failed batches return to production line. QC dept issues Non-Conformance Report.

## Q: Expense reimbursement policy
Submit within 5 working days after return. Receipts required. Over 5000 RMB needs director sign-off.
